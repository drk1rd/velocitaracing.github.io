ABOUT US

Velocita Racing is the exclusive combustion class formula student team of Ramaiah Institute of Technology, Bengaluru, Karnataka. This team comprises of 35 passionate and enthusiastic undergraduate students from various departments, who are continuously working on the development of racing grade vehicles for various competitions across India. The racing platforms such as Formula Bharat has given our students a chance to explore various engineering practices which play a vital role in the development of our country. Our finish line is more than just a place on the track; our mission is and has always been to innovate, to broaden the frontiers of young professionals through their creativity, ingenuity, and dexterity. Our team of highly qualified students is the driving force behind it, each year developing better racing machines, very much in line with the current developments of the automotive industry.

Student careers at velocita

Velocita Racing provides a wide range of experience and opportunity to express your skills and talent.

We cover both technical and non technical aspects of Learning. Stay tuned for our recruitment drive.
We got something for everyone, happy racing!

Explore wide range of area of interests with velocita.

BUSINESS PLAN

Make a business strategy for the car and its production to maximize our profits. Static event which requires high understanding of finances and marketing.

SPONSORSHIP

The department that raises funds for each project, allocates space on the car to sponsoring brands and manages monetary funds.

PHOTOGRAPHY & MEDIA

Be the eyes of the team. Cover the team's journey and create content for documentation and online publicity.

SOCIAL MEDIA

Manage our social media pages to show the world what Velocita Racing is. Requires good understanding of social media platforms and how to get a good reach.

DOCUMENTATION

Write, manage and compose the documents of all departments, from technical to finances. Requires precise writing and organisational skills.

AERODYNAMICS

Department to analyze and design aerodynamic devices to make the downforce required for a quick car. Wings, spoilers, diffusers and more.

BRAKE SYSTEM

Designs and manufactures a high performance brake system with low weight and high efficiency.

CHASSIS

The unit that ties all the sub-systems of a car together. The design needs understanding of structures, car balance and mechanical grip.

ELECTRICAL

The nervous system of the car. Design and manufacture the most minimal and robust electrical system according to regulations.

POWERTRAIN

Design and work on a power producing device, the engine. Getting the most power and efficiently putting it to the ground with minimal loss.

STEERING

Department to make the steering system and most of the driver controls. Requires mechanical understanding and ergonomic knowledge.

SUSPENSION

The department that controls how the car behaves. Most complicated sub system yet the one with highest satisfaction once you hit the optimum grip.
