maya

smaller | lighter | faster "MAYA" or VRF2 is the latest creation of Velocita Racing for the season 2020-21. It's compact, lighter and faster than ever. Moreover it's turbocharged by Garret's GT0612. MRF ZTD1 gives better grips to her. Maya has a push rod type double wishbone independent suspension setup at both front and rear end.
80 Kmph top speed
50 hp turbocharged
