Student Career Areas

Find what suits you the best

NON TECHNICAL ROLES

BUSINESS PLAN

Make a business strategy for the car and its production to maximize our profits. Static event which requires high understanding of finances and marketing.

SPONSORSHIP

The department that raises funds for each project, allocates space on the car to sponsoring brands and manages monetary funds.

PHOTOGRAPHY & MEDIA

Be the eyes of the team. Cover the team's journey and create content for documentation and online publicity.

GRAPHIC DESIGNER

Design our brand products, such as merchandise and more.

SOCIAL MEDIA

Manage our social media pages to show the world what Velocita Racing is. Requires good understanding of social media platforms and how to get a good reach.

DOCUMENTATION

Write, manage and compose the documents of all departments, from technical to finances. Requires precise writing and organisational skills.

TECHNICAL ROLES

AERODYNAMICS

Department to analyze and design aerodynamic devices to make the downforce required for a quick car. Wings, spoilers, diffusers and more.

BRAKE SYSTEM

Designs and manufactures a high performance brake system with low weight and high efficiency.

CHASSIS

The unit that ties all the sub-systems of a car together. The design needs understanding of structures, car balance and mechanical grip.

ELECTRICAL

The nervous system of the car. Design and manufacture the most minimal and robust electrical system according to regulations.

POWERTRAIN

Design and work on a power producing device, the engine. Getting the most power and efficiently putting it to the ground with minimal loss.

STEERING

Design and work on a power producing device, the engine. Getting the most power and efficiently putting it to the ground with minimal loss.

SUSPENSION

The department that controls how the car behaves. Most complicated sub system yet the one with highest satisfaction once you hit the optimum grip.
